Task Description

Your task is to evaluate the quality of a translation from English to Ukrainian in two successive stages: fluency and adequacy.

Stage 1: Fluency Evaluation

You will be shown only the Ukrainian translation. Rate on a 0–100 scale the extent to which the translation is well-formed grammatically, contains correct spellings, adheres to common use of terms, titles, and names, is intuitively acceptable, and can be sensibly interpreted by a native speaker.

Fluency Scale

  • 0–25: Incomprehensible
  • 25–50: Disfluent
  • 50–75: Good
  • 75–100: Flawless

Stage 2: Adequacy Evaluation

After completing the fluency assessment, you will be shown both the English source and the Ukrainian translation. Rate how much of the meaning expressed in the source text is also expressed in the target translation.

Adequacy Scale

  • 0–25: None
  • 25–50: Little
  • 50–75: Most
  • 75–100: All
